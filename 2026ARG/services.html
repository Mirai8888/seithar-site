Services

Sensor Grid

We watch your information environment. Continuously. The scanner reads for active cognitive manipulation across the platforms where your organization, your people, and your brand exist. When someone begins an operation against you, we see it. You get threat alerts, periodic briefings, and the pattern analysis that tells you what is happening before it becomes visible to anyone else.

Threat Mapping

We identify who is operating against you. Network topology. Coordination patterns. Infrastructure. The output is an intelligence brief that tells you how the adversary is organized, which nodes carry the most influence, where the operation is vulnerable, and what it will do next. This is not social media monitoring. It is adversary analysis.

Active Defense

Counter-operations. We design and deploy narrative interventions that neutralize hostile campaigns in progress. The method depends on the threat. Sometimes the correct response is inoculation, pre-exposing the target audience to attenuated versions of the attack narrative. Sometimes it is disruption. Sometimes it is silence. We determine which, execute it, and measure whether it worked.

Persona Operations

Sustained presence in target information environments through synthetic operators. These are not bots. They are coherent identities with behavioral depth, platform history, and social context. They function as persistent assets executing assigned objectives over weeks or months. Positioning, narrative seeding, community engagement, influence mapping from the inside.

Full Spectrum

Everything above, unified into a single campaign. We design the operation, build the infrastructure, deploy the assets, run the engagement, and report the outcome. You define the objective. We handle everything between the objective and the result. This is cognitive warfare as a managed service.

Tell us the problem. We will tell you if we can solve it.
