## SCT Mapping

### SCT-005 (Identity Targeting) — Population-Scale Personalization

"Individually tailored content" is Identity Targeting (SCT-005) automated and scaled. When AI generates unique content for each target based on their profile, the result is millions of simultaneous SCT-005 operations — each calibrated to the specific identity, values, fears, and beliefs of a single individual.

This was previously impossible. Personalized influence operations required human operators who could not scale beyond hundreds of targets. AI removes the scaling constraint. HoleSpawn demonstrates this at the tool level. Nation-states are now deploying it at the infrastructure level.

### SCT-003 (Authority Fabrication) — Fake Account Networks

"Thousands of unique fake accounts" is Authority Fabrication (SCT-003) at industrial scale. Each account manufactures a unique persona — a fictional authority source that the target evaluates as genuine. The uniqueness is critical: duplicate accounts trigger pattern recognition. Unique accounts defeat detection by appearing organic.

### SCT-007 (Recursive Infection) — Real-Time Amplification

"In real time across an entire population" introduces the temporal dimension of SCT-007. The individually tailored content is designed to provoke sharing, discussion, and redistribution — turning each target into an amplification vector for the attacker's narrative. The AI-generated content is the seed. The target population's social behavior is the propagation mechanism.
